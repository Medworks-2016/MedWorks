About Us

We are MedWorks.
We help nurses and doctors bring their innovations to the world.

Our mission is to help medical device stealth innovators, both in the US and abroad, reach a wider audience with their software and hardware hacks. By providing a network of resources and fellow practitioners to these gamechangers, their products are able to earn widescale adoption while attracting interest from VCs and angels from across the globe.
